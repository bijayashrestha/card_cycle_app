01

The Float Window

Every card has a statement close date and a due date (~21–25 days later). Spend right after the close — your purchase won't show up until next cycle, giving you up to ~55 days interest-free.

02

Multi-Card Rotation

Space your cards' close dates evenly (e.g. every 7–10 days). There's always one card in its "fresh" window. Each week, rotate to the card that just closed. More cards = more flexibility.

03

5-Day Alert Rule

This app will notify you 5 days before each due date and again on the day itself. Never miss a payment — a single late payment can trigger a penalty APR and hurt your credit score.

04

Always Pay in Full

Always pay the full statement balance — never just the minimum. Interest charges will instantly cancel all float benefits. Set autopay to full balance on every card.

05

Track Every Spend

Use the Spending tab to log every transaction. The Summary tab shows your monthly breakdown by card and category so you always know where your money is going.
